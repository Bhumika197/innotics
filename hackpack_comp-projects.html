Arduino is a small computer (microcontroller) that you can program to control lights, motors, and sensors. It’s like the brain of a robot, telling it what to do based on the code you write. Many beginners use Arduino to build cool projects like smart home devices, robots, and even musical instruments!

 

Arduino UNO
Components

Ultrasonic Sensor is like a bat’s echolocation system! It sends out sound waves that bounce off objects and return, helping it measure distance without touching anything. It’s used in parking sensors, obstacle-avoiding robots, and even automatic door systems!

 

Ultra-Sonic Sensor
DHT Sensor (Temperature & Humidity Sensor) works like a tiny weather station! It measures the temperature and humidity in the air and is used in weather monitoring, smart greenhouses, and air conditioning systems. It helps keep track of the environment around you!
